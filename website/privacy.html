Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

Last Updated: December 10, 2025

Jot ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Jot iOS application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Notes and Content: All notes, attachments, and associated metadata (folders, tags, version history) are stored locally on your device using the Isar database. We do not access, store, or transmit your notes to our servers unless you explicitly initiate a sync or backup feature.
  • API Keys: If you provide your own AI provider API keys (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini), these are stored securely on your device and are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Feedback & Support: If you contact us for support or submit feedback, we may collect your email address and message content to respond to your inquiry.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymized usage data (feature interactions, error logs, performance metrics) to improve the App. This data does not include note content or personally identifiable information.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, iOS version, and app version for compatibility and debugging purposes.

1.3 Third-Party Services

  • AI Providers: When you use AI features (text generation, summarization, etc.), your prompts and selected note content are sent to the chosen AI provider (Gemini, OpenAI, or Anthropic) via their APIs. These providers have their own privacy policies:
    • Google (Gemini) Privacy Policy
    • OpenAI Privacy Policy
    • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Advertising (Google AdMob): If you opt in to ads, we use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:
    • Device identifiers (IDFA, if you consent via Apple's App Tracking Transparency)
    • IP address, app usage data, and interaction with ads
    • AdMob's privacy policy: Google Privacy Policy

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (local note storage, version history, search, etc.)
  • Process AI requests when you explicitly initiate AI features
  • Display ads (only if you have not opted out) and track ad impressions for credit granting
  • Improve the App through anonymized analytics
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may share data in the following circumstances:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: AI providers (when you use AI features) and Google AdMob (when you consent to ads).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred (you will be notified).

4. Your Data Rights and Choices

4.1 Local-First Storage

Your notes are stored only on your device. You can export, delete, or back them up at any time through the App's settings.

4.2 Ad Consent & Opt-Out

You can disable ads entirely in Settings → Ad Settings → Opt Out of Ads. If you consent to ads, you will see a consent dialog (powered by Google's User Messaging Platform) before any ads are shown. You can revoke consent at any time.

4.3 AI Feature Control

You control which AI providers are used and can disable AI features entirely. If you use your own API keys, you are directly responsible for compliance with the provider's terms.

4.4 Data Deletion

To delete all app data, uninstall the App or use the Settings → Storage → Clear All Data option. This will permanently delete all local notes, attachments, and settings.

4.5 Access & Correction

Since your data is stored locally, you have full access and control. To request a copy of data we may have collected (e.g., support emails), start a discussion in our community hub.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: Backups can be encrypted using AES-256-GCM. API keys are stored securely in iOS Keychain.
  • Local Storage: Notes remain on-device and are protected by iOS's sandboxing.
  • HTTPS: All network requests (AI APIs, ad serving) use HTTPS.

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Jot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us, and we will delete it.

7. International Users

The App is designed primarily for iOS users. If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the U.S. or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to this transfer.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-app notification or by updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • GitHub Discussions: Ask a question
  • GitHub Issues: Report a bug

10. Ad Technology Providers & App-Ads.txt

Jot uses Google AdMob to serve advertisements. To comply with ad industry transparency standards, we maintain an app-ads.txt file at:

https://larryalexander.github.io/jot/app-ads.txt

This file lists authorized ad sellers for our app. For more information, see the IAB Tech Lab's App-Ads.txt specification.

This privacy policy is provided for informational purposes. For legal advice, consult an attorney.
